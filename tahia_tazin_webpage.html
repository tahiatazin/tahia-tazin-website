About Me

I am Tahia Tazin, currently pursuing a Master's in Engineering at the Department of Human Intelligence Systems, Kyushu Institute of Technology, Fukuoka, Japan. My research areas include Applied Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Activity Recognition, Ubiquitous Computing, Ubiquitous Healthcare, and IoT.
